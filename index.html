Hiking the trails of MacRitchie Reservoir

MacRitchie Reservoir's myriad attractions makes it a playground of nature for those who love the outdoors.

Arab Street is home to some of Singapore's most eye-catching street art, alleys of hip cafes, intriguing bars as well as quaint shops and boutiques
